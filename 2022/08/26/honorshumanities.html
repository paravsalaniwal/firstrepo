Unit 1: Art as a perspective

8/26/2022 Notes

  • Art doesn’t have an actual definition as people can view it from their perspective and the emotions they feel expressed at the moment

  • Art used to be seen as more from an aesthetics side, but is now seen more for its importance and emotions it can reveal from one’s body

  • Art can also be seen as a great way of communicating through feelings and life stories of each individual
